Quick, cheap and healthy recipes

Do you not like cooking in a common kitchen because it is usually dirty? Or are just a very
busy student who needs easy, cheap and healthy recipes? You are in the right place.
WRITTEN BY: OLIWIA OLENDER
18 OCTOBER 2021

Overnight oats

INGREDIENTS:

  • 60g rolled oats
  • 100ml milk
  • 130g yoghurt of your choice (I used bluberry-banana flavoured one)
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • 1 banana

INSTRUCTIONS

1) Mix oats, milk, yoghurt and honey together until combined.

2) Place your overnight oats in a fridge for the night or at least 6 hours.

3) Before eating cut a banana and place the pieces on the oats.

Pasta with feta cheese and tomatoes

INGREDIENTS:

  • 500g pasta
  • 500g cherry tomatoes
  • 150g feta cheese
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 30ml olive oil
  • salt, pepper, oregano

INSTRUCTIONS

1) Cook the pasta and set aside.

2) Place everything except for pasta in a baking tray and put in an oven heated up to 200 degrees Celsius for 30 minutes

3) Mix the pasta with the sauce together.
